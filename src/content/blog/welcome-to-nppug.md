## Welcome to NPPUG!

We're excited to launch the Norfolk Power Platform User Group - a community for Microsoft Power Platform enthusiasts across Norwich and Norfolk.

### Our Mission

**Connect, learn, share, and grow together.**

Whether you're just getting started with Power Apps, building complex Power Automate flows, creating stunning Power BI reports, or exploring the possibilities of Copilot Studio - you're welcome here.

### What We Offer

- **Regular Meetups**: Monthly events featuring technical demos, guest speakers, and plenty of networking
- **Inclusive Community**: We welcome all skill levels, from curious beginners to enterprise experts
- **Free Events**: Our meetups are always free, complete with pizza and refreshments
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Learn from real-world use cases and share your own experiences

### Get Involved

There are many ways to participate:

1. **Attend our meetups** - Check the Events page for upcoming sessions
2. **Become a speaker** - Share your knowledge with the community
3. **Connect online** - Follow us on LinkedIn for updates
4. **Spread the word** - Tell your colleagues about NPPUG

### Looking Ahead

We have exciting plans for 2025 and beyond, including the **East of England Power Platform Summit** - a two-day conference celebrating our regional Power Platform community.

Stay tuned for more updates, and we hope to see you at our next event!
